About Me

My name is Dennis Perez. I have 2 daughters and am recently married. I currently work at Children's Hospital Los Angeles. My role is linking the children and families we see at the hospital to services in the community. Particularly in school. My specialty is school advocacy for children with special needs. I have over 12 years experience providing advocacy to children with developmental disabilities. I am currently enrolled in a coding boot camp through UCLA extension. I am hoping to find a new career in computer coding. This is an exciting journey and am looking forward to the opportunities it presents.



Please feel free to visit my projects through GitHub.
